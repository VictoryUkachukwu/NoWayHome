Legal Practice Areas

Election Petition

An election petition is a legal challenge to the validity of an election result, alleging irregularities or any instance of fraud.

Criminal Law

Criminal law prosecutes individuals for offenses against the state, punishing wrongdoers and protecting the larger society.

This law balances national security and humanitarian needs, and regulates foreign entry and citizenship.
